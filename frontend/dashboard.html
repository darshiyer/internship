🛠️ Data Construction Flow
1
Source Acquisition
Manually scraped legislative government content + legal corpora ingestion (Samanantar, MILPaC, Futurebee, AIKosh).
2
Text Standardization
Unicode normalization, whitespace cleanup, punctuation harmonization, script-level cleanup for Indic text.
3
Language and Domain Filtering
Retained legal-domain rows using language-ID checks, legal-keyword density filters, and malformed-row removal.
4
Cross-Lingual Alignment
Built multilingual alignment via exact + loose + fingerprint English-anchor matching to form quadruplet rows.
5
Direct Pair Extraction
Extracted MR↔KN direct pairs from aligned rows and deduplicated to produce effective training inventory.
6
Evaluation Packaging
Prepared internal legal splits and external benchmark mapping for automatic + human evaluation pipelines.
Overlap definitions are different by construction rule: strict English-text intersection is lower, while quadruplet alignment uses exact, loose, and fingerprint matching and therefore yields higher aligned coverage.
